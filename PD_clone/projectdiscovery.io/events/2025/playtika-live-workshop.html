Building your risk-oriented AppSec program

Explore the dynamic world of application security and learn how to stay ahead of evolving threats. Join us as we delve into the critical importance and guidance of how to build your application security program. We'll walk through how Playtika's product security group secures their organization's dynamic systems against vulnerabilities and how they make informed decisions by integrating security considerations into early stages of the software development lifecycle.

  • Navigating the evolving cybersecurity landscape

    Stay ahead of emerging threats with actionable insights.
